Spooky Ghost Race

This is a spooky racing game just in time for the spooky season. Race the two ghosts along the scary path. I wonder who is going to win? One ghost uses the arrow keys to move while the other uses the WAD keys to move. Click on the space bar for a super spooky song while you're playing!

Run Away From the Ghosts

Oh no the ghosts are coming to get you! Your character will follow your mouse so help them escape. When the a ghost catches you it will freeze and tell you how long it took. Move your mouse over the pants or over the dress and your character will change!
